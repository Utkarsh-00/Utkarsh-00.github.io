AI RESEARCH SCIENTIST


Utkarsh’s analytical bent of mind and curiosity led him to play with data and discover the roots which govern the business industry today. Manipulating data to extract meaningful information, he has been working as a Data Scientist ever since. His knowledge and experience on the subject have paved various opportunities in the form of technical talks and the subject matter of his books.

BLOCKCHAIN EVANGELIST


Being a believer of revolutionary technologies, Utkarsh has ardently followed Blockchain technology which he believes is transforming the world of digital finance and assets. His passion for innovation and vision for efficient data management led him to be a prolific blockchain evangelist.

COMMUNITY WORKER


Utkarsh has been a mentor to several developers as well as business communities around the world. Be it Product Design or Technology, his experiences, and understanding of the new opportunities propelled him to support creative initiatives.
